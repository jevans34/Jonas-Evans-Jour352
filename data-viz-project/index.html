Time Period When All 3 Played on Tour

Djokovic, Nadal, and Federer dominated ATP rankings throughout most of their careers. This chart compares their rankings to each other at the end of each year, throughout the 15 year period that all 3 were playing.

Big 3 Grand Slam Wins (2006-2021)

Broken Down By Tournament

By Jonas Evans

Grand Slams are the four most important tournaments of the ATP season. The Big 3 lead all other players in Grand Slam wins, however their win breakdowns are very different. Nadal was dominant in France, while Djokovic had much more success in Australia. Federer, of course, made his mark as the king of grass at Wimbledon.
